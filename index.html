01.1 The coworking accelerator

The entitlement engine is the heart of the platform.

Standard CRM cannot manage monthly free-hours quotas, proration, carryover, real-time consumption and an auditable ledger. Volge builds this as a purpose-built Dataverse solution — the central custom component behind mandatory FIN-04, with overage rating for FIN-05 and room booking for APP-05.

FIN-04 · Custom

Free-hours quota & ledger

Monthly quota per contract, proration and configurable carryover, with real-time consumption and an auditable entitlement ledger.

FIN-05 · Custom

Location-specific overage

Hours beyond entitlement are rated at each location's price and tax, generating invoice lines in real time or in a controlled batch.

APP-05 · Custom

Room booking workflow

Availability, free-hours deduction, overage estimate or approval, calendar invites and cancellation rules — tied to the ledger.

06 / 06 Commercial model

Modular by design. Remove a module, see the impact.

Requirement PRP-01 asks for modular pricing with the cost impact of removal made explicit; PRP-02 for two commercial models. Both are addressed. Index units below are illustrative; final figures are in the priced schedule.

Model A · Subscription (SaaS)

Managed service on Microsoft subscriptions

Nestwork holds Dynamics 365, Power Platform and Azure subscriptions by user tier and module; Volge provides the managed service.

  • Licences by user tier & module
  • Volge managed service & support SLA
  • Contract term & annual escalation
Model B · Accelerator licence

Volge accelerator, perpetual or source-available

Volge licenses its coworking accelerator & IP on a perpetual or source-available basis with annual maintenance, on subscriptions held by Nestwork.

  • Accelerator licence + customisation rights
  • Maintenance %, upgrade & support policy
